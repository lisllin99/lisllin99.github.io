Troubled with dark eye circles?

Dark eye rings can make a person look tired, aged and haggard. There are many causes of dark eye circles, it could be due to shadows that are casted by eye bags, tear troughs or puffy eyelids.

How can we treat dark eye circles?

Here at Miracle Aesthetic, we provide treatments which utilises creams and medications to improve allergies and eczematous skin conditions. These ointment and prescription are designed by our doctors.

On top of which, we also provide laser treatments to tackle pigmentation issues. Pigmentation is common cause for dark eye circles. The laser will be able to break up pigmented particles or melanin deposited in the under eye area. To get the best result, at least three laser sessions are required. Our laser treatment package contains three appointments which can be arranged upon further consultation.

Fillers are also available in our clinic. When applied to the under eye area, the fillers will plump up thinning skin which reduces the visibility of the under lying vessels. In addition, these fillers can help to smoothen out the area, which will reduce any shadow forming due to eye bags or sunken tear troughs. One filler session is sufficient to decrease the dark eye circles immediately. Under eye fillers typically last between 4-6 months*.
